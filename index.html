Our Story

Why Jagranvi?

Our name comes from Jagraon, a historic market town in the Ludhiana district of Punjab, established in 1688 by Rai Kamaluddin of Raikot. For centuries, Jagraon has been known as an important agricultural and trading center with deep roots going back to the Mughal era, and it later became a notable town in the story of India's freedom struggle , home to freedom fighter Lala Lajpat Rai.

Our family has belonged to Jagraon for generations. Our ancestors served as Shahi Tabeeb (royal physicians) to the Mughal court, and that tradition of herbal healing has been passed down through our family ever since. The name Jagranvi carries that town with us , it identifies us as the people who trace their roots, and their craft, back to Jagraon.

Today, we are the 7th generation continuing this legacy. From the beginning until now, we have relied on 100% natural, organic herbs, and we follow the same slow, traditional preparation process our forefathers used , because real healing takes time, and we believe in a process that works gently, without harsh side effects.
